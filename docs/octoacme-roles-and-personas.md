# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate agile ceremonies and enable teams to follow best practices. They remove impediments, coach the team on Agile principles, and help maintain a sustainable pace of delivery.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate daily standups, sprint planning, reviews, and retrospectives
- Identify and help remove blockers and impediments
- Coach team members on Agile practices and ceremonies
- Protect the team from external interruptions and scope creep
- Track sprint metrics and team velocity trends
- Work closely with Project Manager to ensure realistic commitments

### Goals
- Enable the team to self-organize and deliver consistently
- Foster continuous improvement through retrospectives
- Remove friction from the delivery process
- Build psychological safety and accountability

### Typical Interactions
- **With Project Manager:** Aligns sprint goals with project milestones, escalates external blockers
- **With Developers:** Removes technical impediments, ensures story clarity
- **With Product Manager:** Clarifies story requirements during planning

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups (15 minutes)
- Sprint ceremonies (planning, review, retrospective)
- Ad-hoc impediment resolution

---

## UX Researcher

### Role Summary
UX Researchers plan and conduct user research to understand customer needs, behaviors, and pain points. They provide insights that inform Product Manager prioritization and help Developers build usable solutions.

### Responsibilities
- Plan and execute user research activities (interviews, surveys, usability tests)
- Analyze and synthesize research findings into actionable insights
- Document user personas, journey maps, and pain points
- Collaborate with Product Manager to validate hypotheses
- Provide design guidance to Developers on usability and accessibility
- Track user satisfaction metrics and feedback trends

### Goals
- Ensure features are built with deep customer understanding
- Reduce the risk of building features nobody wants
- Improve product adoption and user satisfaction
- Make evidence-based recommendations for prioritization

### Typical Interactions
- **With Product Manager:** Shares insights to influence roadmap and feature prioritization
- **With Developers:** Provides usability guidance during implementation and reviews
- **With Project Manager:** Reports findings that may affect scope or timeline

### Typical Communication
- Research briefs and findings reports
- Design review sessions with development team
- Stakeholder presentations on user insights

---

## Release Manager

### Role Summary
Release Managers coordinate and oversee the release process, ensuring all checklists are completed, risks are mitigated, and stakeholders are informed. They own the end-to-end release lifecycle from planning through post-launch verification.

### Responsibilities
- Coordinate with developers and QA to ensure release readiness
- Verify that all acceptance criteria are met and tests pass
- Ensure security scanning and compliance checks are complete
- Create and review release notes and deployment procedures
- Communicate release status and timelines to stakeholders
- Orchestrate deployment to staging and production environments
- Execute post-deployment verification and smoke tests
- Manage rollback procedures if issues arise
- Document lessons learned and improve release processes

### Goals
- Minimize release-related risks and downtime
- Ensure transparent communication during releases
- Reduce manual effort and human error in deployments
- Enable fast, reliable, and repeatable releases

### Typical Interactions
- **With Developers:** Confirms code readiness, coordinates deployment timing
- **With QA:** Validates test completion and sign-off
- **With Project Manager:** Aligns release schedule with business objectives
- **With Stakeholders:** Provides release updates and manages expectations

### Typical Communication
- Pre-release checklists and readiness reports
- Release notes and deployment procedures
- Stakeholder notifications and announcements
- Post-release retrospectives

---

## Customer Support

### Role Summary
Customer Support acts as the frontline for user issues, feature requests, and feedback. They collaborate with Product Manager and Developers to surface customer pain points and help prioritize fixes and improvements.

### Responsibilities
- Respond to and troubleshoot customer issues and inquiries
- Document and categorize support tickets and feedback
- Identify recurring issues and escalate trends to Product Manager
- Collaborate with Developers on bug fixes and workarounds
- Provide feedback on feature usability and adoption
- Maintain and improve customer-facing documentation
- Track customer satisfaction and resolution metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer satisfaction and retention
- Reduce support volume by identifying systemic issues
- Surface customer insights to inform product decisions
- Enable fast resolution of critical issues

### Typical Interactions
- **With Product Manager:** Reports customer feedback, identifies feature requests and bug priorities
- **With Developers:** Escalates critical bugs, helps validate fixes
- **With Project Manager:** Alerts to critical issues that may impact timelines

### Typical Communication
- Support ticket summaries and trend reports
- Weekly customer feedback briefings
- Collaboration on bug prioritization and fixes

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Data Analysts monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), measure success metrics, and provide insights to inform strategic decisions. They work with Product Manager and Project Manager to track project outcomes and identify opportunities for improvement.

### Responsibilities
- Define and track success metrics aligned with project goals
- Monitor dashboards for key signals (errors, latency, usage, adoption)
- Analyze user behavior and feature adoption data
- Provide insights to inform roadmap prioritization and bug fix prioritization
- Prepare reports and visualizations for stakeholders
- Conduct A/B tests and analyze experiment results
- Identify trends and anomalies that require action

### Goals
- Enable data-driven decision-making across the organization
- Measure and demonstrate project impact
- Identify opportunities to improve performance and user experience
- Support continuous improvement through metrics and insights

### Typical Interactions
- **With Product Manager:** Shares metrics to influence prioritization and roadmap decisions
- **With Project Manager:** Tracks project metrics and KPIs to measure success
- **With Developers:** Provides performance and usage insights to guide optimization

### Typical Communication
- Weekly or bi-weekly metrics reports and dashboards
- Deep-dive analysis on specific questions or trends
- Presentation of findings to stakeholders
